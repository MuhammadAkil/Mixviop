Empowering Businesses Through Smart Communication

At Mixvoip, we provide advanced telecom, internet, and cloud solutions tailored to help businesses grow and stay connected across Luxembourg, Europe, and beyond.

From Local to Global

Mixvoip began in 2008 with a simple idea to make business communication easier, more powerful, and more personal. Over the years, we've grown from a local team in Luxembourg to an international company serving thousands of clients in more than 15 countries. Our growth is rooted in innovation, customer care, and a belief that communication is the heart of every successful business.
